Fly Private to the Most Popular Southern European Destinations

Southern Europe is the region most people picture when they think of private jet travel. The Greek islands, the Croatian coast, the Balearics, the Italian Riviera: the appeal is obvious. What is less obvious until you have experienced it is how poorly commercial aviation serves many of these places in practice. Reaching Hvar, Formentera, or Capri without a combination of flights, ferries, and transfers is genuinely difficult. Flying privately often means landing closer to your final destination than any airline itinerary could manage.

LunaJets has a dedicated office in Madrid and covers the full breadth of Southern Europe, from Lisbon and the Algarve to the Eastern Aegean. Business trips into Madrid, Milan, Athens, or Belgrade sit alongside leisure charters to the islands and ski flights to Courmayeur or Baqueira-Beret. Our advisors know the regional airports well, including the smaller ones that make island and mountain access genuinely direct.
