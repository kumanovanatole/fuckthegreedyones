Fly Private to the Most Popular Western European Destinations

Across Western Europe, an hour or two of flying separates Alpine ski resorts, sun-drenched coastlines, historic city centres, and some of the world's busiest financial hubs. Flying private here means touching down at Courchevel or Davos rather than connecting through Geneva, arriving in Ajaccio or Calvi instead of Marseille, or covering London, Frankfurt, and Zurich in a single working day. The region also has hundreds of smaller airfields well beyond the reach of commercial airlines, and that is precisely where private aviation comes into its own.

LunaJets has operated in Western Europe since its founding in Geneva in 2007, and this remains the heart of what we do. With offices in Geneva, London, Zurich, Paris, and Madrid, our advisors know these markets well: the aircraft that suit each route, the airports worth considering over the obvious alternatives, the seasonal patterns that shape availability. Whether you need a last-minute ski weekend, a multi-stop business trip across several capitals, or a summer escape to Corsica or the Riviera, we will present the right options and manage every detail from there.
