Fly Private to the Most Popular Destinations in North America

North America is the world's largest private aviation market by a considerable margin. The United States has more private airports than any other country, and the distances between cities, combined with the concentration of high-demand leisure destinations, make charter the practical choice for a broad range of travellers. The ski resorts of Colorado and Wyoming, the beaches of Palm Beach and Miami, the entertainment and business circuits of New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, and San Francisco: the private airport network reaches all of them without the constraints of commercial scheduling.

LunaJets arranges transatlantic charters from Europe and the Gulf into the US and Canada, as well as domestic legs for international clients already on the ground. Our advisors are well versed in US and Canadian operational requirements and plan each trip with the same level of detail as any European route. Whether the destination is Teterboro for New York, Aspen, Jackson Hole, or Whistler in British Columbia, we handle the arrangements from departure to landing.
